Afritada Recipe

Afritada the best tomato dish

Afritada is a tomato sauce dish for the filipinos. It tastes differentiate to other filipino dishes since it uses tomato sauce as its primary ingridient for taste. This dish also have good nutrients in it as it includes carrots thats good for the eyes and potatoes thats good for digestion.

Ingridients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepper
  • Bell Pepper

Steps in Cooking Afritada

  1. Sauze the garlic until it turned to golden brown.
  2. Add the chicken and wait for it to turn into golden brown
  3. Add some water in and wait for it to boil.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and let it boil for 5mins
  5. Add the Carrot and potatoes
  6. Enjoy!

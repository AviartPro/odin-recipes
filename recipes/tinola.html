Tinola Recipe

Tinola the soup of Filipino

Tinola is a very easy cook for filipinos. It only contains a lot of cheap ingridients and a very common meat that is a Chicken. A lot of filipinos like the tinola as a food for lunch and even evenings. When it's raining, that's where the tinola comes place. A very cold weather will make Tinola standout as you sip its hot soup.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Malunggay Leafs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Cooking Oil
  • Pepper
  • Squash
  • MSG

Steps in Cooking Tinola

  1. Sauce the garlic and wait for it to cook.
  2. After the garlic is brown, put the chicken in.
  3. Wait for the chicken to finish cooking and then add some water.
  4. After the water is done boiling. Put the malunggay leaf.
  5. Lastly put the cut squash in the dish and wait for it to cook.
  6. Enjoy!

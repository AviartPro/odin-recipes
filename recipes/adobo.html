Chicken Adobo

Filipino food

Chicken Adobo is one of the most common food here in the Philippines. It was loved by alot of Filipinos because of its cheap ingridients and popular taste to them. Almost 90% of the filipinos preferred the Pork as their meat as this gives a lot of protein inside the dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Lagundi Leafs
  • Vinegar
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Water

Steps in cooking Chicken Adobo

  1. Prepare the Cooking pot and heat it.
  2. Put the Oil and Garlic in and sauze it.
  3. Once the garlic cooked, Put the raw chicken and mix it.
  4. After the chicken is slightly cooked. Put all the water.
  5. After it boils. Season it using the soy sauce and vinegar.
  6. Wait for it to finish cooking and you can tell this is done if the water thickens.
  7. Enjoy!
